Ensembles

There will be two ensembles included during these nights.

Philharmonic Orchestra

The Philharmonic Orchestra at LaGuardia is a group composed of all orchestral instruments that contains mostly junior and senior instrumental majors, led by Mr. Joseph Meyers.

Senior Chorus

Senior Chorus is a group made up of many students, also mostly juniors and seniors. They will be led by Ms. Jana Ballard.
